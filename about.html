About Us

Welcome to our Virtual CPU Scheduling Lab! Dive into the dynamic world of CPU scheduling algorithms, where efficiency and performance optimization are the keys to enhancing system responsiveness and resource utilization.

Our Mission

In the realm of operating systems, CPU scheduling algorithms play a crucial role in determining the order in which processes are executed, thereby influencing system performance. Our mission is to provide an interactive platform for learning and experimentation with various CPU scheduling algorithms.

What we offer

Explore CPU Scheduling Algorithms

Our virtual lab offers an immersive environment where you can explore a range of CPU scheduling algorithms. From classic algorithms like First-Come, First-Served (FCFS) and Shortest Job First (SJF) to modern variations like Shortest Remaining Time First (SRTF), Longest Job First (LJF), Round Robin (RR), and Priority-based scheduling (PP), the possibilities are vast.

Customizable Scenarios

Tailor your experiments to suit your learning objectives. Adjust parameters such as process arrival times, burst times, quantum time (for RR), and priority levels (for PP). Explore how changing these factors affects the behavior and performance of the scheduling algorithms.

Educational Resource

Whether you're a student delving into the fundamentals of operating systems or an educator seeking interactive teaching tools, our virtual lab serves as a valuable educational resource.
